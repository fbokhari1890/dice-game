Rules

Automatic Win: When a player rolls a 4-5-6 he will automatically wins.

Automatic Loss:When a player rolls a 1-2-3 he will automatically loses.

Triples: When a player rolls the same number on all dice (ex.5-5-5). Higher triples beat lower trips, so 4-4-4 is better than 3-3-3.
